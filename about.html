Our Mission

Since 2020, MBAbookings has been dedicated to providing seamless booking experiences in Francistown, Botswana. Our mission is to connect visitors and locals alike with the best hotels, event venues, and restaurants, ensuring every experience is infused with the warmth and hospitality of Botswana. We strive to make every booking effortless, reliable, and tailored to your needs, celebrating the rich culture of our community.

About Francistown

Francistown, known as the "Capital of the North," is Botswana’s second-largest city, located 400 km from Gaborone. Founded in 1897 as a gold mining town, it holds a rich history and serves as a vibrant hub for commerce and culture in northern Botswana. Visitors can explore attractions like the Supa Ngwao Museum, which showcases Setswana heritage, or enjoy the scenic beauty of the Tati River. Francistown is also home to lively events like the Francistown Agricultural Show and the Domboshaba Cultural Festival, making it a perfect destination for cultural immersion.
